== Overview

You can notify and alert administrators using SMTP server.

== Create an email notification channel

. link:{{ '/am/current/am_userguide_authentication.html' | relative_url }}[Log in to AM Console^].
. Click *Settings > Alerts > Notifiers*.
. Click the plus icon image:{% link images/icons/plus-icon.png %}[role="icon"].
. Select *Email* as your notifier type and click *Next*.
. Give your notifier a name.
. Configure the settings.
. Click *Create*.

NOTE: You can customize the default `body` text with notification properties.
